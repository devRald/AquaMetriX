Automated Water Quality Detection

AquaMetriX is an innovative mobile application that is used in helping to protect the environment through water quality monitoring. This is based on a real-time notification for increased awareness in preventing the risk of water pollution. It is connected to an Arduino device wherein the data being gathered will directly be sent to the application.

How It Works

Step 1

Power on the device

The device has an input of 12V and an output of 5V. Make sure not to give an input higher than 12V to the device or else it will explode.

Step 2

Wi-Fi/LAN Connection

Connect the device to your internet same with your smartphone/laptop/desktop to synchronize and monitor your device.


Step 3

Test Proper

Place the device on the water to initially start the water quality testing and monitoring.


Step 4

Track Measurement

Open the web/mobile app to track the measurement by looking at the logs and notifications tab of the application.
